Storefront

This project is meant to closely mimick the needs of a storefront. A persistent database is used to create and store clients, carts, products, and suppliers as well as the relationships between the entities. For example, the union between a product and a supplier is a product currently available for purchase, and the union between that union entity and a cart is a product ready for purchase. Interconnected frontend services are used to track, update, and display database information to clients.
